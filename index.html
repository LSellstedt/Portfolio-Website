About Me

Hi, I'm Linus Sellstedt, a UI & Tools Programmer who enjoy facing great challenges when it comes to the question "how can we simplify this?". I've been studying game development for about 5 years now using various engines. Most commonly Unity but as of late I've been making all of my projects in Godot.

I've always been a big fan of games and gaming but never imagined a future where I'd be making them until a few years ago when I started my developer journey. When that started I thought I would be stuck as a Unity developer for life but then I found out about PlaygroundSquad in Falun. They made me start broadening my game engine usage and I'm so incredible thankful for that.

Having worked in so many engines now I feel more ready than ever to get out there and work in the game industry!
